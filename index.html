Iceland boasts a superb range of day and night attractions in one travel destination. This inspiring 40K sq. mile island contains glaciers, icebergs, active volcanoes, and seasonal northern lights. The country’s fascinating wildlife includes puffins and world famous Icelandic horses. Not to be outdone by nature, man has made a substantial contribution as well. Iceland’s renowned Viking history is a top tourist attraction. Historic architecture and quaint thatched houses dot the countryside. Activites include sightseeing, kayaking, ice climbing, and a vibrant music scene. The Langjökull Glacier was recently an innovative venue for the Secret Solstice music festival. Accommodations include an igloo hotel, where guests can view the northern lights, and sleep under the stars. Day or night, Iceland is sure to inspire the adventurer in each of us.

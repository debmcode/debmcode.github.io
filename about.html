About

The inspiration for DayNight, Vol. 1 Iceland began with a conversation I had years ago with a coworker. While en route to London, her sister’s flight made a layover stop in Iceland. The report was that Reykjavík had a great nightlife scene. I thought it was very interesting that a visitor could go ice climbing during the day, and to a rave at night. Ever since that chat, Iceland is on my future travel wish list.

This is a mock travel site created for an intro to HTML and CSS class. Project Code Phase 1 is part of the TechConnect program offered by the New York Public Library. Information on this site is anecdotal, and is my personal take on the subject. Please note that some images are used for illustration purposes only.
